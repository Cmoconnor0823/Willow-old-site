Family owned and operated for over 30 years, the Blue Willow is known for serving fresh, wholesome food. Whether looking for a hearty breakfast with mimosas or a delicious dinner of pasta or fresh seafood and a glass of wine, Blue Willow's innovative yet familiar menu offers nostalgic American classics that comfort and gratify.

Voted Tucson's Best Breakfast and Best Outdoor Dining for nine years, and known for serving breakfast all day, Blue Willow is a Tucson tradition. Enjoy year-round patio dining in the lush garden or have a candlelit dinner in the charming, converted 1930 adobe house.
